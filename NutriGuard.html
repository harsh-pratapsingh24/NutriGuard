About Us

At NutriGuard, our mission is to promote awareness about misleading food marketing practices. We aim to help people make informed decisions about their diet and health by exposing unhealthy marketing gimmicks and suggesting healthier alternatives. Together, we can support SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being by encouraging sustainable and transparent practices in the food industry.

Information

Sugary Breakfast Cereals

Marketing Gimmick: Advertised as "low-fat" but contain high sugar levels.

Better Alternative: Oats with fresh fruits or unsweetened granola.

Flavored Yogurts

Marketing Gimmick: Marketed as "high-protein" but loaded with added sugars.

Better Alternative: Plain Greek yogurt with natural sweeteners like honey.

Fruit Juices

Marketing Gimmick: Labeled as "100% natural" but often contain preservatives and added sugar.

Better Alternative: Freshly squeezed juices or whole fruits.

Energy Bars

Marketing Gimmick: Claimed to be "healthy snacks" but are calorie-dense and high in sugar.

Better Alternative: Homemade energy bars or nuts and seeds.
